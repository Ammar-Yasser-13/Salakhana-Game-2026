# _*Game project*_

⩺ project contents:
1. player movement
2. hitboxes and collision
3. UI and menus
4. enemies
5. core gameplay/gameloop
6. level designing
7. assets and animation

1) _*Player movement:*_
* walk
* run
* slide
* jump
* shoot
* adjusting the player speed to the fast paced environment of he game
* shooting cooldown between each bullet

2) _*hitboxes and collision*_
* player collision with floors and walls
* player bullet killing an enemy and damaging the player by 1 health point
* enemies bullet damaging the player by 1 health point
* health pick ups that give the player 1 health point

3) _*UI and menus*_
⩺ main menu:
* level select
* quick toturial on how to play
* best level clear times -> optional
* sittings -> if there is
⩺ UI:
* player health points
* current number of enemies and number of all enemies in the level
* current number of health packs and number of all health packs in the level
* current number of bullets in the gun and a realoding icon when reloading
* mini map that show enemies and the player -> optional

4) _*enemies*_
* tracking the player postion to shoot him
* shooting cooldown between each bullet
* rotating to look toward the player
* die when they get shot

5) _*gameloop*_
* the  player must clear the level by killing all enemies in the level while maintaining atleast one health point to clear the level
* the player loses 1 health point each time he succsusfuly kills an enemy  
* a recorded timer will be displayed of the best time taken to clear the level and the player can type three letters to that record -> optional

6) _*Level designing*_
* the level should atleast take 1 to 2 mintues to be cleared by a gamer and 3 to 4 by casuals
* simple and not too complex 
* clear enemies positions and not hidden fully by walls
* to be fun will navigating by using the movement mechaincs of the player
